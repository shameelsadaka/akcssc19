About

IEEE All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention

AKCSSC '19, the fifth edition of All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention will be hosted by St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai. With an expected count of 200 participants, AKCSSC’19 is themed on “The Evolution of Technology” and will be an unprecedented congregation of diverse speakers from across the globe.

Venue

St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai

SJCET Palai is an Indian private college managed by the Syro-Malabar Catholic Diocese of Pala, located in Pala. It is an AICTE approved college offering professional degree programs in engineering and management courses .SJCET Palai is a constituent college of Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam and APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. It promotes all techno cultural events conducted to improve the overall indusive personality of the student. As the motto puts forward 'A college with a Difference is what SJCET Palai is and will always be.
